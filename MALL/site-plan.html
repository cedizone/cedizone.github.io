Overview

Purpose

CediMall is an online shopping mall with the best business concept. We give members the opportunities to buy products from our platform at wholesale price, CediMall sells the products the members bought at wholesale price at Retail price to consumers all over the world. CediMall pays members every 12 days and gifts products freely to members as well. Note: CediMall is not a pure network marketing company. This means you are mandated to recruit people before you make money, but if you do so, you get extra money.

Audience

Wholesalers, Retailers, Investors, and everybody who needs a part time work.
